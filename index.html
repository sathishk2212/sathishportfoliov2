How it started

Was a Tech fanatic, so i've learned photoshop when i was around 15 to make headers for blogs and all, continued doing it till B.Tech until a friend asked me to join a professional forum for designing.

Joining a college magazine forum really helped me grow as a professional in the design field alongside gathering a huge audience for my work.

I stepped into the world of Motion Graphics when I worked for TEDxVBIT as a lead designer. With loads of confidence and experience under my belt, I applied for jobs and got into Swing Set Branding later that year which focussed on social media marketing. Gradually, I started freelancing too.

After a few months at SSB, I wanted to explore more and joined Lead Thoughts which takes care of almost all the marketing needs of a company. UI/UX was an additional skill I gained here as I worked on a few projects. I have started gaining interest in web development too.
